About Us

The Equal Opportunity Cell (EOC), Sri Venkateswara College is a social service society under the aegis of the University of Delhi. Established in 2013, the Cell has been operational for over a decade, working extensively in the pursuit of social justice, inclusivity and accessibility for differently-abled students.

Our society comprises the volunteers and PwD students. We intend to reduce the gap between differently-abled students and the mainstream society. EOC provides academic assistance, mentorship and support while also promoting opportunities for holistic growth, social engagement, and skill development.

EOC has been at the forefront of initiatives such as scribing support for examinations, organizing inclusive events and celebrations, conducting adventure and educational trips, hosting motivational speaker sessions, skill-development workshops, and inclusive sports activities, among many others. Through these efforts, we aim to create an accessible, inclusive and welcoming environment where every student feels valued, empowered, and able to realize their potential with dignity.

Our mission is to enable differently-abled students to achieve their potential with dignity, confidence, and independence, while also cultivating a culture of inclusivity within the broader college community.
