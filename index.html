Our Vision

At the Equal Opportunity Cell (EOC), Sri Venkateswara College, we envision a campus where equality, accessibility, and inclusivity are the foundation of student life. Our vision is to empower differently-abled students by creating opportunities that nurture their academic, personal, and social growth, while also fostering empathy and responsibility among our volunteers. We aim to nurture a campus where diversity iscelebrated, barriers are broken, and every student can access opportunities and realize their potential.
  • Provide academic assistance and study aid to support learning.
  • Arrange scribes for examinations, ensuring equal opportunities.
  • Organize inclusive events, sports, and activities that celebrate diversity.
  • Conduct interactive sessions and skill-enhancement workshops for the holistic development of both PwD students and volunteers.
  • Improve mobility and accessibility within the college, offering proper guidance and infrastructural support to PwD students. Through these efforts, we aim to bridge the gap between differently-abled students and mainstream society, ensuring that every student can learn, participate, and thrive with dignity.
